Privacy Policy for SplitVibe
Last Updated: April 9, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how SplitVibe ("we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, and shares your personal information when you use our mobile application.

By using the App, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy. This policy is designed to comply with the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) and South Africa’s Protection of Personal Information Act (POPIA).

1. Information We Collect
A. Personal Data Provided by You
When you register or use SplitVibe, we collect the following information:

Username: To identify you within the community.

First and Last Name: To personalize your experience.

Email Address: To manage your account, send notifications, and provide support.

B. Device Permissions
To provide the core features of SplitVibe, we request access to:

Camera: We use the camera permission to allow you to take photos directly within the app.

Image Storage (Files and Media): We require access to your device’s storage to save photos you take or to allow you to upload existing images from your gallery.

2. How We Use Your Information
We use the collected data for the following purposes:

To create and maintain your user account.

To facilitate the core functionality of the app (e.g., capturing and sharing images).

To communicate with you regarding updates or technical issues.

To comply with legal obligations.

3. Lawful Basis for Processing
GDPR: We process your data based on Consent (Art. 6(1)(a)) and the Performance of a Contract (Art. 6(1)(b)) to provide the services you signed up for.

POPIA: We process your personal information in accordance with Section 11 of POPIA, based on your voluntary consent and the necessity of the data for the app's functionality.

4. Data Storage and Security
Your data is stored securely. We implement industry-standard technical and organizational measures to protect your personal information against unauthorized access, loss, or theft.

Retention: We only keep your personal data for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide you with the app's services.

5. Your Rights (GDPR & POPIA)
Depending on your location, you have the following rights regarding your data:

Right to Access: You can request a copy of the data we hold about you.

Right to Rectification: You can request that we correct inaccurate information.

Right to Erasure ("Right to be Forgotten"): You can request that we delete your account and all associated data.

Right to Object: You may object to the processing of your data for specific purposes.

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us at the email address provided below.

6. Third-Party Services
We do not sell your personal data to third parties. If we use third-party services (such as cloud storage or analytics), we ensure they are compliant with relevant privacy laws and only handle data necessary for their specific function.

7. Children's Privacy
SplitVibe is not intended for use by children under the age of 13 (or 18 in South Africa without parental consent). We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

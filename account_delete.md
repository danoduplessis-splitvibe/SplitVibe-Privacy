## Account Deletion & Data Retention
Users have the right to request the deletion of their account and all associated personal data at any time.

**How to request deletion:**
To delete your SplitVibe account, please send an email from your registered email address to **daniel.splitvibe@gmail.com** with the subject line "Account Deletion Request."

**What data is deleted:**
Upon processing your request, we will permanently remove:
* Your profile information (Username, First Name, Last Name, Email).
* Any images stored in our database/storage associated with your account.

We will process all deletion requests within 30 days, in compliance with GDPR and POPIA regulations.
